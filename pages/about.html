About AFZ

Empowering persons with albinism in Zambia through advocacy, awareness, and unwavering support since our founding.

Our Mission

To advocate for the rights, dignity, and inclusion of persons with albinism in Zambia through awareness campaigns, policy advocacy, support services, and community empowerment programs.

Our Vision

A Zambia where persons with albinism enjoy and exercise their rights and fundamental freedoms on an equal basis with the rest of society.

Our Values

Dignity, Equality, Inclusion, Empowerment, Transparency, Accountability, and Community-centered approach in all our endeavors.

Our Impact

500+ Members Supported
15+ Active Programs
10 Provinces Reached
8 Years of Service

Our Journey

2016

Foundation Established

AFZ was founded by passionate advocates to address the challenges faced by persons with albinism in Zambia and create a unified voice for change.

2018

First Major Campaign

Launched our first nationwide awareness campaign, reaching over 50,000 people across Zambia and starting crucial conversations about albinism.

2020

Policy Advocacy Success

Successfully advocated for policy changes that improved access to healthcare and education for persons with albinism in Zambia.

2022

Program Expansion

Expanded our programs to include vocational training, entrepreneurship support, and comprehensive healthcare initiatives.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched our digital platform to better serve our community and expand our reach through technology and online advocacy.
